Relay Races in the Czech Republic and Europe

Discover upcoming triathlon, swimrun and endurance relay events across the Czech Republic, Slovakia and the wider European calendar. Each listing carries the event date, location, disciplines and team size.

Spring season highlights

The spring calendar opens with road and trail relays around Brno and Prague, followed by open-water swimrun events on the Moravian lakes. Most accept teams of two to four athletes.

How team relays are scored

In a standard relay each leg is timed individually and summed into a team total; mixed and age-graded categories apply separate classifications so clubs can compare like with like.

Registration and logistics

Entry usually opens three months ahead and closes when capacity is reached. Organisers publish start lists, course maps and transponder pickup windows on each event page.
